About KBN College

Kakaraparti Bhavanarayana College (Autonomous) is a distinguished institution and one of the best outcomes of a century-old illustrious charitable organization, S.K.P.V.V. Hindu High Schools Society, which for the last 117 years has been providing exceptional education through its eight reputable academic institutions.

It is one of the pioneering educational institutions in Vijayawada run with the motto "TEJASWINAVADHITAMASTU", which means "May our Study be Enlightening".

The college is known for its perseverance and commitment to academic excellence and value-based holistic education.
